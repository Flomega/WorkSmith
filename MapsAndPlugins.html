1 How to use Lands

Claiming Land
Create a Land:
Use /lands create [name] to establish a new land.
Claim Chunks:
Execute /lands claim to claim the chunk you're in.
Selection Mode:
For larger areas, use /lands selection to select corners, then /lands claim to claim the area.

Managing Lands
Access Menu:
Open the land menu with /lands.
Edit Specific Land:
Use /lands edit [land] to specify which land to manage.

Player Permissions
Trust a Player:
/lands trust [player] [area]
Untrust a Player:
/lands untrust [player] [area]
Toggle Permissions:
Open the land menu with /lands. Navigate to the roles section. Adjust permissions as needed.

Promote/Demote Players:
Open the land menu. Go to the players section. Shift + left-click to promote, shift + right-click to demote.

3 How to use lands

Sub Areas
Creating a Sub Area:
Open the land menu with /lands menu. Click on the "Areas" item. Click "Add Area" and enter a name in chat. Select the area's boundaries. Assign the selection with /lands selection assign [area].

Managing Sub Areas
Trust Players:
Assign specific players to sub areas.
Set Flags:
Define permissions and behaviors within the sub area.

Rent & Sell System
Set Up Rental:
Use /lands rent to configure rental options.
Manage Tenants:
Monitor and manage current tenants through the land menu.

Selling Land
List for Sale:
Use /lands sell to put land up for sale.
Complete Sale:
Finalize transactions through the land menu or commands.
